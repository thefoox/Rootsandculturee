<objective>
Implement the complete customer purchase flow: localStorage-persisted shopping cart (drawer + full page), Stripe Elements checkout with PaymentIntent, Stripe webhook for order fulfillment, Resend email confirmations, and confirmation modal. Both guest and authenticated checkout supported.

Purpose: This is the core e-commerce transaction pipeline. Without it, the storefront is read-only. Every product page gets a working "Legg i handlekurv" button, customers can browse their cart, pay inline via Stripe Elements, and receive order confirmation.

Output: Working end-to-end purchase flow from "add to cart" through payment to order confirmation email.
</objective>
